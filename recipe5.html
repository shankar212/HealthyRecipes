Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu

Estimated Calories: 280

Health Benefits: Rich in vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, green onions

Instructions:

  1. Press tofu to remove excess water, then cube it.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add minced garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add assorted vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  5. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Serve over cooked brown rice or quinoa.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions if desired.
  8. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu!
