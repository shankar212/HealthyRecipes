Grilled Chicken Wrap

Estimated Calories: 350

Health Benefits: Lean protein source.

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat wrap
  • Vegetable toppings (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers)
  • Greek yogurt or tzatziki sauce for dressing

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with olive oil, cumin powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken until fully cooked.
  3. Warm the whole wheat wrap.
  4. Assemble the wrap with grilled chicken and vegetable toppings.
  5. Drizzle with Greek yogurt or tzatziki sauce.
  6. Roll the wrap and serve.

Enjoy your delicious and healthy Grilled Chicken Wrap!
