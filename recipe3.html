Idly

Classic and soft South Indian steamed rice cakes, perfect for a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup idly rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak idly rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked urad dal and fenugreek seeds to a smooth batter.
  3. Grind the soaked idly rice to a coarse batter and mix it with the urad dal batter.
  4. Add salt and allow the batter to ferment overnight.
  5. Grease idly plates, pour the batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes until idlies are cooked.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Calories: 70 per idly

Health Benefits: Low in calories, a good source of carbohydrates and protein.
