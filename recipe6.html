Indian Dal Tadka

Estimated Calories: 220

Health Benefits: Excellent source of protein and essential nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash lentils and cook with turmeric until soft.
  2. In a separate pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste. Saute until onions are golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  5. Add garam masala, salt, and cooked lentils. Mix well and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice or Indian bread.
  8. Enjoy the delicious Indian Dal Tadka!
