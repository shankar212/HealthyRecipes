Fruit Smoothie Bowl

Enjoy a refreshing and nutritious fruit smoothie bowl to kickstart your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend the mixed berries and banana until smooth.
  2. Layer the smoothie in a bowl.
  3. Top with Greek yogurt, granola, and drizzle with honey.
  4. Enjoy your delicious fruit smoothie bowl!

Calories: 250 per serving

Health Benefits: Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber.
